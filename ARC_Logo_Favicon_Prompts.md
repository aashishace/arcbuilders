# ARC Builders — Logo & Favicon Generation Prompts

## Business Context
- **Company**: ARC Builders (Aesthetic Residential and Commercial)
- **Industry**: Premium residential & commercial construction, Queensland Australia
- **Target Audience**: Indian-Australian NRI families, professionals, and investors
- **Brand Colours**: 
  - Primary Gold: #D2AF26 (vibrant luxury gold)
  - Dark: #1a1a1a (near-black charcoal)
  - Light: #fafafa (warm off-white)
- **Brand Personality**: Premium, trustworthy, luxury, precision, modern yet culturally respectful
- **Tagline**: "Builders with a difference"

---

## LOGO PROMPTS

### Prompt 1 — Primary Logo (Full)
```
Design a premium, modern logo for "ARC Builders" — a luxury residential and commercial construction company in Australia. The logo should feature the letters "ARC" as the dominant mark with "BUILDERS" as a secondary wordmark below or beside it. Style: minimalist, architectural, premium. The "A" could subtly incorporate a roofline, building structure, or arch element. Use a colour palette of deep charcoal (#1a1a1a) and vibrant luxury gold (#D2AF26). The design should feel like a high-end property developer or premium builder brand. Clean lines, geometric precision, no gradients. Transparent background. Suitable for dark and light backgrounds. Vector style, scalable.
```

### Prompt 2 — Monogram / Icon Mark
```
Design a premium monogram icon for the letters "ARC" for a luxury construction company called ARC Builders. The monogram should work as a standalone icon/favicon. Style: geometric, architectural, minimalist. The letters should interlock or overlap elegantly. Could incorporate subtle building/arch/structural elements. Colours: gold (#D2AF26) on charcoal (#1a1a1a) background, OR gold standalone on transparent. The icon should be recognisable at very small sizes (16x16 to 64x64). No text other than the A-R-C letterforms. Vector style, clean edges.
```

### Prompt 3 — Logo with Indian-Australian Cultural Nod
```
Design a sophisticated logo for "ARC Builders" — a premium builder in Australia whose clients are predominantly Indian-Australian families. The logo should subtly blend modern Australian architectural design with a refined Indian aesthetic touch — perhaps through a subtle arch (reminiscent of both modern architecture AND traditional Indian archways), geometric patterns inspired by jali/lattice work, or a lotus-inspired element integrated into the letterform. Keep it minimal and luxury — not overtly ethnic. Colours: charcoal (#1a1a1a) and gold (#D2AF26). "ARC" as the main mark, "BUILDERS" secondary. Transparent background, vector style.
```

### Prompt 4 — Horizontal Layout (For Navbar)
```
Design a horizontal logo for "ARC Builders" optimised for website navigation bars. Left-aligned, compact height (max 48px display height). "ARC" in bold with an architectural icon mark integrated, "BUILDERS" in lighter weight alongside or below. Colours: gold (#D2AF26) and white, designed to sit on a dark/transparent navigation bar. Minimalist, premium, clean. No background. Vector/SVG style.
```

## FAVICON PROMPTS

### Prompt 5 — Favicon (Square Icon)
```
Design a square favicon/app icon for "ARC Builders" construction company. The icon should be the letters "ARC" or just "A" in a geometric, architectural style. Must be clearly legible at 16x16, 32x32, and 192x192 pixels. Use gold (#D2AF26) on a dark charcoal (#1a1a1a) square background with slightly rounded corners. Minimalist, bold, premium. The letterform could incorporate a subtle roofline or arch. No fine details that would be lost at small sizes.
```

### Prompt 6 — Favicon Variant (Gold on Transparent)
```
Design a minimal square favicon for "ARC Builders". A single geometric "A" letterform with an integrated arch or roofline element, in gold (#D2AF26) on a transparent background. Must read clearly at 16x16 pixels. Bold strokes, no thin lines. Architectural, modern, luxury feel.
```

## USAGE NOTES

1. **Generate in these tools**: Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Adobe Firefly, or Ideogram
2. **After generation**: Vectorise the chosen design using Vectorizer.ai or Adobe Illustrator's Image Trace
3. **File formats needed**:
   - Logo: SVG (for website), PNG at 2x resolution (fallback)
   - Favicon: ICO (16x16, 32x32, 48x48), PNG (192x192 for Android), Apple Touch Icon (180x180)
4. **Place files at**:
   - Logo SVG: `/public/logo.svg`
   - Favicon: `/public/favicon.ico` (replace existing)
   - Apple Touch Icon: `/public/apple-touch-icon.png`
   - Android Icon: `/public/icon-192.png`
