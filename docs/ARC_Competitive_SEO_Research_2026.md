# ARC Builders Competitive SEO Research - 2026

Research date: 22 May 2026

## Search Landscape

Observed Australian/Brisbane custom-home competitors and SERP patterns:

- `fifth+co` ranks around Brisbane South bespoke intent with a narrow luxury positioning, named projects, local areas and QBCC trust detail.
- `NQ Build` presents broader Queensland authority with commercial/residential breadth, testimonials and multi-region service language.
- `Emerald Developments` has a dedicated `custom-home-builder-brisbane` page with long-form service copy, transparent quotes, experience, FAQs/areas and direct CTAs.
- `Endura Homes` leans into boutique Brisbane builder positioning, QBCC licence, planning/design/build and South East Queensland coverage.
- `JR Construction` uses a strong portfolio grid with repeated suburb labels like Brisbane Southside, Tarragindi and Camp Hill.
- `OMNI Built Homes` competes with “#1 home builders Brisbane” language, transparent pricing and one-on-one service.

## What ARC Should Beat Them On

- Local depth: keep building out suburb pages for Brisbane Southside, Logan, Rochedale, Calamvale, Pallara, Greenbank, Eight Mile Plains, Mount Gravatt, Bahrs Scrub and South East Queensland.
- Real proof: every project should link to a relevant location page and include suburb, scope, gallery alt text and a “discuss similar build” CTA.
- Trust: keep QBCC licence visible, add client review snippets to location pages after approval, and encourage Google reviews that mention suburb and project type.
- Differentiation: lead with custom homes, renovations, premium family residences, transparent pricing and quality-first delivery. Keep Vastu as a secondary capability, not the main snippet.
- Crawl strength: footer links should expose priority service areas and core services from every page.
