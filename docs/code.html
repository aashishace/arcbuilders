Explicit Exclusions

The following items are not factored into the standard build contract and must be calculated as separate variations if required:

  • cancel Landscaping & Fencing: Retaining walls beyond 500mm, turf, plants, and boundary fences.
  • cancel Council Escalations: Specialist permit fees or unexpected council infrastructure levies.
  • cancel Rock Excavation: Removal of underground rock formations if encountered.
  • cancel Pool Installation: Excavation, shell, and equipment for swimming pools.
