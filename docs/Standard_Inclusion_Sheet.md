# ARC Builders Standard Inclusion Sheet

Version: 1.0
Audience: All residential clients
Purpose: One clear, transparent inclusion sheet that explains exactly what is included, what is excluded, and where allowances apply.

## 1) Planning Summary

### Goals
1. Make inclusions easy for every customer to understand.
2. Remove ambiguity between included items and upgrade options.
3. Reduce disputes by documenting allowances, assumptions, and exclusions clearly.
4. Use one standard format for website display, proposal PDFs, and contract pre-check.

### Rollout Plan
Phase 1: Finalize standard structure and language.
Phase 2: Convert all current client-specific sheets to this format.
Phase 3: Publish on website with expandable sections and FAQ.
Phase 4: Add acknowledgement step before quotation acceptance.
Phase 5: Review quarterly and update version number.

Customer visits Inclusion Sheet

Variation Control Flow
  Scope request
    -> Is it in Included list?
       -> Yes: no variation cost
       -> No:
          -> Is there an allowance?
             -> Yes: adjust by allowance difference
             -> No: full variation quote required
          -> Client written approval
             -> Schedule and cost update

## 5) Standard Inclusion Content (Draft Master Text)

### 5.1 Plans, Compliance and Warranty
Included:
- Engineering certification for frame and trusses.
- Standard construction drawings and required compliance documentation.
- Statutory insurances where applicable.
- Standard maintenance period and structural warranty period (as per contract and law).

Clarifications:
- Final authority and utility charges depend on council, utility providers, and site constraints.
- All warranties are subject to contract terms, maintenance conditions, and legislation.

### 5.2 Site Works and Earthworks
Included:
- Standard site preparation for a buildable platform within defined fall/tolerance.
- Standard excavation and leveling assumptions.

Not included unless stated:
- Abnormal rock removal, contaminated soil treatment, retaining walls, dewatering, traffic control, or access constraints.

### 5.3 Slab and Structural Frame
Included:
- Engineer-designed slab and structural system for standard site classification assumptions.

Allowance/assumptions:
- Soil classification assumptions must be stated clearly.
- If piers or upgraded footing are required due to actual report/site condition, variation applies.

### 5.4 Roofing and External Envelope
Included:
- Standard roof, fascia, and gutter profile from builder range.
- Insulation as required by approved energy assessment.

### 5.5 Windows and External Doors
Included:
- Standard aluminum windows/doors with required locks.
- Standard glazing type unless higher spec required by regulation or specified upgrade.

### 5.6 Internal Linings and Carpentry
Included:
- Standard plasterboard/villaboard in wet areas.
- Standard cornice, skirting, architraves, internal door style from builder range.

### 5.7 Flooring and Wall Finishes
Included:
- Main area flooring from builder range.
- Bedroom flooring allowance per sqm where applicable.

### 5.8 Kitchen and Pantry
Included:
- Base and overhead cabinetry per approved plan.
- Standard benchtop thickness/material category.
- Appliance package from nominated builder range.
- Splashback tile from nominated range.

### 5.9 Bathrooms, Ensuite and Laundry
Included:
- Standard sanitaryware, tapware, vanity, shower screen, and tile coverage as specified.
- Laundry trough and standard mixer/taps.

Clarification:
- Decorative feature tiles, specialty tapware, and custom joinery are upgrades unless explicitly included.

### 5.10 Electrical, Data and Lighting
Included:
- Main switchboard and safety devices as required by standards.
- Standard count of lighting and power points as per electrical plan.
- NBN/data provision point as per plan.

Clarification:
- Smart home systems, CCTV, security alarms, and additional feature lighting are upgrades unless listed.

### 5.11 Plumbing and Hot Water
Included:
- Internal plumbing rough-in and fit-off for included fixtures.
- Standard hot water unit type and capacity from builder range.
- Standard external tap points as listed.

### 5.12 Heating, Cooling and Insulation
Included:
- Insulation to meet energy report requirements.
- Air-conditioning system specification (capacity and zones) where listed in contract inclusions.

### 5.13 Painting and Final Handover
Included:
- Internal and external paint system to standard specification and coverage.
- Builder clean and site tidy prior to handover.

## 6) Exclusions Section (Must Be Explicit)

Unless specifically listed as included, the following are excluded:
- Council/authority cost escalations after quote date.
- Landscaping, fencing, letterbox, clothesline, curtains/blinds, and loose furniture.
- Decorative lighting fixtures beyond batten/downlight package.
- Specialty finishes, custom metalwork, premium stone, premium appliances.
- Additional site costs due to latent conditions.

## 7) Variation and Allowance Rules (Client-Friendly)

1. If item is Included: no extra charge.
2. If item has Allowance: charge or credit is difference from allowance.
3. If item is Excluded: full variation cost applies.
4. No variation proceeds without written approval.
5. Variation may affect timeline as well as cost.
