# ARC Builders Digital Growth Playbook

This document is the operating guide for ARC Builders' social media, blog, SEO, and content-led lead generation.

## 1. Brand Direction

### Public-facing brand language

- Use `Brisbane & South East Queensland` in visible website copy, footer copy, and primary social bios.
- Keep the tone premium, clear, trustworthy, and family-oriented.
- Lead with `custom homes`, `transparent pricing`, `quality-first construction`, `Vastu-aware planning`, and `multi-generational living`.

### Hidden SEO targeting

- Keep targeting `Logan`, `Rochedale`, `Eight Mile Plains`, `Pallara`, `Calamvale`, `Mount Gravatt`, `Greenbank`, and `Brisbane South` in metadata, blog SEO fields, and local landing content.
- This gives ARC Builders local search coverage without forcing every suburb into visible brand messaging.

### Core audience segments

1. Custom home families in Brisbane south and South East Queensland.
2. Indian-Australian families seeking Vastu-aware and multi-generational homes.
3. Owner-build clients comparing custom design vs project-home compromises.
4. Investors and NRI buyers who want transparent communication and predictable build management.
5. Commercial clients needing fitouts, medical centres, and specialist construction.

## 4. Content Strategy That Drives Website Traffic

### Main objective

Use social content to create attention, trust, and repeat visibility, then move viewers to:

1. Project pages.
2. Blog articles.
3. Contact page.
4. WhatsApp or direct enquiry.

### Content pillars

#### Pillar 1: Project proof

- Completed home walkthroughs.
- Before-and-after transformations.
- Room-by-room highlights.
- Construction progress reels.
- Materials and finish details.

#### Pillar 2: Education

- Cost myths.
- Build process breakdowns.
- Contract comparisons.
- Inclusions guidance.
- Site selection and planning mistakes.

#### Pillar 3: Trust and authority

- Client testimonials.
- Founder camera-to-phone messages.
- Team introductions.
- On-site quality checks.
- Explaining how transparent quoting works.

#### Pillar 4: Lifestyle and cultural fit

- Vastu planning content.
- Multi-generational home design tips.
- Practical family layouts.
- NRI build-management guidance.

#### Pillar 5: Commercial expertise

- Medical centre fitout insights.
- Shop fitout progress.
- Compliance and delivery discipline.
- Commercial planning mistakes to avoid.

#### Pillar 6: Podcast snippets

- Builder advice clips.
- Myth-busting clips.
- Client journey clips.
- Design/planning conversations.

### Best performing formats for ARC Builders

1. Short vertical videos with a strong first 2 seconds.
2. Carousel posts with practical education.
3. Before-and-after visuals.
4. Founder talking-head clips.
5. Testimonial quote cards with real project images.
6. Blog teaser posts driving to the website.

### Weekly publishing cadence

#### Instagram + Facebook

- 3 reels per week.
- 2 carousel posts per week.
- 3 to 5 story frames on posting days.
- 1 testimonial or trust post each week.

#### LinkedIn

- 2 authority posts per week.
- 1 project/commercial case study per week.

#### YouTube

- 1 short per week.
- 2 longer videos per month.

#### TikTok

- 3 short-form clips per week using Instagram reel source material.

#### Blog

- 1 high-intent article per week minimum.
- 2 per week if content production allows.
